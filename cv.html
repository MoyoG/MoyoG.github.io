About us

download cv

Munkhjargal Gochoo was born in Ulaanbaatar City, Mongolia, in 1984. He received B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electronics Engineering from the Mongolian University of Science and Technology in 2004 and 2005, respectively. He stayed at Department of Electronics, Mongolian University of Science and Technology as a Lecturer during 2005-2011. In 2017, he got his Ph.D. degree from National Taipei University of Technology in electrical engineering field. During 2017-2018, he was a Research Assistant Professor at Department of Electronic Engineering, National Taipei University of Technology, Taipei, Taiwan. During 2018-2019, he worked as a post-doctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, National Taiwan Ocean University, Taiwan. Currently, he is working as an Assistant Professor at Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering, College of Information Technology, United Arab Emirates University, UAE. Prof. Gochoo is a Visiting Professor at Department of Electrical Engineering, National Taipei University of Technology. He is a Co-PI of AI and Robotics Lab, UAEU. His main research interests are deep learning, artificial intelligence, social robotics, e-health, eldercare, IoT, wearable systems, and computers.
